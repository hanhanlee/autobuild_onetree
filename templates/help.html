Quick guidance for daily Autobuild tasks.

Quick FAQ
Common questions and answers.

Pinned jobs are protected. Unpin the job first, then you can prune or delete it.

Use "Prune Workspace" on old jobs to remove the work/ directory, or use Storage Maintenance in Settings to clean sstate and downloads.

Open the job detail page. The Artifacts section lists output files and provides download links.
Icon Legend
Meaning of common actions.
  • Pin job Keep on top and protect
  • Prune workspace Delete work/ directory
  • Delete job Remove job and files
  • Retry job Run again in place
Artifacts
Where outputs appear.

Artifacts are collected after a job finishes and appear on the job detail page.

Common extensions: .bin, .img, .mtd, .static.mtd
Storage Maintenance
Manual cache cleanup.

Settings includes a Storage Maintenance section to clean sstate and downloads.

SState cleanup removes files unused for X days (atime). Downloads cleanup only removes root files and preserves git repos.
